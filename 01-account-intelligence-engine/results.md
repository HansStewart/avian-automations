***

Output Quality Assessment

All 15 companies received complete outreach packages including a cold email opener, a LinkedIn connection request message, and three phone call talking points. Every output passed the Quality Critic Agent on the first attempt, with no regeneration cycles required during this run. Average outreach quality score across all 15 companies: 7.9 out of 10.

The highest scoring output was for 3M, which received a 9.1. The research returned strong OSHA-specific signals and a documented thermal incident, giving the Personalization Agent precise, verifiable details to reference. This reflects the system's dependency on research signal strength: richer research produces more specific, higher-scoring outreach.

***

Limitations of Demo Dataset

The 15 companies in this dataset were selected to represent a range of ICP fit levels. They are not a random sample of the industrial sector. Hot tier results may be proportionally higher in this demo than in a live account list populated from a broader prospecting source. A live deployment with a raw prospecting list would be expected to produce a higher proportion of Warm and Cold results with a smaller Hot tier percentage.
